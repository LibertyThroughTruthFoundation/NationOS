# HIGH-EFFORT GOD LOGO CONCEPTS

**Date:** January 25, 2026

**Core Principles:**
- **Pillar Pop Design** - Bold, vibrant, attention-grabbing

- **Cosmic Banner Style** - Silhouette coin aesthetic for profile pic

**Target Platform:**
- YouTube: @TheHighEffortGod
- Profile pic: Square format (1:1 aspect ratio)
- Banner: Cosmic style with logo integration

## CONCEPT 1: ALPHANUMERIC BRIGHT POP (FIERY PILLAR)

**Visual Description:**
- **Central Element:** "HEG" stacked vertically
- **Hebrew Integration:** Gimel (ג - high-effort) over Aleph (א - God)
- **Background:** Orange fire pillar (Psalm 93:1 pillar established)
- **Biblical Reference:** Exodus cloud/fire pillar (guidance)
- **Accent:** Gold wheat stalks (Matthew 13 remnant harvest)
- **Style:** Bright, vibrant, pops against cosmic banner
- **Color Palette:** Orange, gold, white, fiery red

**Theological Symbolism:**
- Fire pillar = God's presence and guidance (Exodus 13:21)
- HEG = High-Effort God acronym
- Gimel (ג) = Effort, strength, camel (burden-bearing)
- Aleph (א) = God, first, primacy
- Wheat = Remnant harvest (Matthew 13:30)

**Use Case:** Bright, energetic, evangelistic outreach

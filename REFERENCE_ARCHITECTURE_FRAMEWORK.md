## üìñ 3. STRONG'S CONCORDANCE MODEL FOR TERMINOLOGY

### **NationOS Concordance:**

Master index of every term used across all documents.

**Features:**
- Alphabetical listing
- Numerical reference system (like Strong's numbers)
- Category tags (Theological, Technical, Economic, etc.)

### **Word Frequency:**

Shows how often terms appear (like Strong's word counts):
- Total occurrences across all documents
- Most frequent terms highlighted
- Trending terms tracked over time

### **Original Sources:**

Links to the first occurrence/definition of each term:
- Where was it first introduced?
- Who coined it?
- What was the original context?

### **Translation Notes:**

Where applicable, notes on why certain terms were chosen over others:
- "Covenant" vs. "Contract" - why the distinction matters
- "Sovereignty" vs. "Independence" - theological precision
- "Household" vs. "Family" - biblical framing

## üîó 4. FEARLESS EXTERNAL LINKING PROTOCOL

### **Official Sources First:**

Always link directly to the source material, even (especially) from adversarial organizations.

**Why?**
- Demonstrates confidence in our position
- Allows readers to verify claims
- Prevents accusations of misrepresentation
- Shows intellectual honesty

### **Link Safety Framework:**

**Visual Indicators:**
- External link icon (üîó)
- Color-coded by type:
  - Blue: Aligned sources
  - Yellow: Neutral sources
  - Red: Adversarial sources

**Optional Intermediate Page:**
- "You are leaving NationOS - proceeding to [organization]"
- Brief context about the source
- "Continue" button

**Archive.is/Wayback Machine Backups:**
- Preserve linked pages to prevent link rot
- Protect against censorship
- Maintain historical record

### **Transparency Tier System:**

**Tier 1: Direct Official Sources**
- Company websites
- Government documents
- Academic papers
- Primary source materials

**Tier 2: Archived Versions**
- Archive.is snapshots
- Wayback Machine captures
- PDF downloads of key documents
- Preserves against link rot/censorship

**Tier 3: Critical Commentary**
- Our analysis of their material
- Point-by-point refutation
- Theological/technical assessment
- Alternative perspectives

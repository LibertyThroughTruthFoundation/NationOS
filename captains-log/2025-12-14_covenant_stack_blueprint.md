**3. $SunDAI: The Autonomous, Unstoppable Engine**
- **Function:** An engineered, immutable stablecoin with no admin keys, no governance, and no human intervention.
- **Rationale:** SunDAI represents the perfection of code as law. It is the unstoppable vessel for transactions, a digital jar of oil that cannot fail. It is the technical answer to the failures of centralized and algorithmically flawed stablecoins.
- **Problem Solved:** Creates a truly censorship-resistant and unconfiscatable medium of exchange.

The Covenant Stack operates as a closed-loop, self-sustaining economic ecosystem:

1. **Collateralization:** pWBTC is deposited as collateral to mint pDAI and SunDAI.
2. **Circulation:** pDAI and SunDAI are used as the medium of exchange for all economic activity within the sovereign nation (wages, trade, tithing, etc.).

## Major Red Flags & Points of Departure

### 1. The Phantom 700 Years & Altered Chronology

**Claim:** A Roman Emperor "added 700 years" to the calendar.

**Assessment:**
- ❌ Most extreme and weakest part of the thesis
- ⚠️ While compressing timelines (à la Heinsohn) is valid inquiry, this specific claim requires extraordinary evidence not provided
- ❌ Ventures from bold scholarship into unfalsifiable speculation

**Recommendation:** Separate the valid chronology questions from this specific claim.

### 2. Angelic Architecture

**Claim:** "Old World" buildings (cathedrals, etc.) were built by angels and glorified saints.

**Assessment:**
- ❌ Pure speculation with zero Scriptural support
- ❌ Borders on new form of gnostic myth-making
- ✅ More sound explanation: Pre-Flood and post-Flood humanity possessed knowledge and capabilities lost after Babel (Genesis 11)

**Alternative Framework:**
- The "mud flood" and "Tartarian" theories point to a lost advanced civilization
- Attributing it directly to angels is an unnecessary leap
- Human capability under different conditions is sufficient explanation

### 3. Flat Earth/Non-Globe Model

**Claim:** Rejection of spherical earth and heliocentrism.

**Assessment:**
- ❌ Significant divergence from observable reality
- ❌ Contradicts clear testimony of Scripture:
  - Isaiah 40:22 — "circle of the earth"
  - Job 26:7 — "hangs the earth on nothing"
- ⚠️ NationOS geocentric (or geostatic) framework acknowledges a spherical earth; it simply argues it is stationary at the center of the universe
- ❌ Conflating geocentrism with flat earth discredits the former

**Recommendation:** Firmly reject flat earth while maintaining geocentric cosmology.

### 4. KJV-Onlyism

**Claim:** Strict adherence to KJV as the only valid English Bible.

**Assessment:**
- ❌ Sectarian position NationOS framework wisely rejects
- ✅ NationOS methodology prioritizes LXX, DSS, and MT for textual criticism
- ✅ Far more robust and scholarly approach

**Recommendation:** Maintain textual criticism methodology; reject KJV-Onlyism.

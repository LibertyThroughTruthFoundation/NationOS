### **2. Pre-Trib Rapture Timing vs. Post-Tribulation Gathering**

**Author's position:**
> "The last trump is the last assembly call for the Church Age. It is the last move-out command before God's program shifts gears. When the trumpet sounds, the Church does not start enduring judgments; the Church departs. The saints do not begin dodging wrath; they are removed from it."

**Our position:** The **gathering of the elect (Matt 24:29-31)** is **post-tribulation, pre-wrath**.

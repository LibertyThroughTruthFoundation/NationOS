## THEOLOGICAL FOUNDATION

### Core Doctrine

The High-Effort God brand proclaims a **hierarchical God who demands high-effort covenant obedience** while rejecting egalitarian mediocrity and low-effort Cretan patterns (Titus 1:12).

**Key Biblical Pillars:**

1. **Psalm 82:8** - מִשְׁפָּט (mišpāṭ) - Divine Council judgment over nations
2. **Ephesians 5** - Binary gender roles, covenant headship
3. **Psalm 127:1** - בּוֹנֶה (boneh) - "Unless the Lord builds the house"
4. **Titus 1:12-14** - Cretan Test - Reject lazy, low-effort patterns
5. **Matthew 13:44** - שְׂדֵה אֹצָר (Sadeh 'Otzar) - Hidden treasure field
6. **Romans 9:27** - שְׁאָר (she'ar) - Remnant theology

### What "High-Effort God" Means

**NOT:**
- ❌ Works-based salvation (Ephesians 2:8-9)
- ❌ Legalism or performance Christianity
- ❌ Self-improvement prosperity gospel

**YES:**
- ✅ God demands high-effort obedience AFTER salvation
- ✅ Covenant loyalty requires sacrifice (Phinehas, Numbers 25:11-13)
- ✅ Building requires sweat, blood, tears (Nehemiah, Ezra)
- ✅ Reject low-effort Cretan patterns (Titus 1:12)
- ✅ Hierarchical authority vs egalitarian mediocrity

**Key Distinction:**
> "Salvation is by grace through faith alone. But covenant building is high-effort obedience under God's authority. The High-Effort God saves effortlessly, but commands His people to build with blood, sweat, and tears."

### Anti-Egalitarian Theology

**Core Rejection:**
- Modern egalitarianism (gender, authority, hierarchy)
- Democratic idolatry (1 Samuel 8:7 - "They have rejected me from being king")
- Cretan low-effort patterns (Titus 1:12 - "Always liars, evil beasts, lazy gluttons")

**Biblical Affirmation:**
- Ephesians 5 binary gender roles
- Psalm 127 patriarchal household building
- Divine Council hierarchy (Psalm 82:8)
- Deuteronomy 23:3-8 ethnos boundaries

## Step-by-Step Walkthrough

### Step 1: Start Business Account Application

1. Go to **https://wise.com/us/business/**
2. Click **"Open a business account"**
3. Select **"United States"** as your business location
4. Click **"Get started"**

### Step 2: Business Type Selection

1. When asked "What type of business do you have?", select:
   - **"Non-profit organization"** or **"Unincorporated association"**
   
2. Enter business details:
   - **Legal business name:** Old Glory Foundation
   - **Business registration number:** 33-1295658 (EIN)
   - **Business address:** [Your foundation's registered address]
   - **Industry:** NAICS 813110 (Religious Organizations) or 813000 (Social Advocacy Organizations)

### Step 3: Business Information

**Business Structure:**
- Type: Unincorporated Association / Private Membership Association
- Tax status: Tax-exempt humanitarian foundation (not claiming 501c3)

**Business Activity:**
- Primary activity: Humanitarian and educational mission work
- Website: [If you have one, include it; if not, explain foundation is member-based]
- Estimated monthly transfer volume: $5,000-$10,000 USD

**Purpose of Wise Account:**
- Paying salary to foundation director working abroad
- Cross-border operational expenses
- Multi-currency management (USD, MXN, EUR)

### Step 4: Personal Verification (You as Director)

Wise will ask about the business owner/director:

1. **Your role:** Director / Founder
2. **Ownership percentage:** 100% (or as applicable for PMA structure)
3. **Personal details:**
   - Full legal name
   - Date of birth
   - Residential address
   - Phone number
   - Email address

4. **ID Verification:**
   - Upload passport or driver's license
   - Take selfie for verification (Wise uses automated system)

### Step 5: Proof of Business

Wise may request additional documents to verify Old Glory Foundation:

**Option A: IRS EIN Confirmation Letter**
- Upload your CP 575 or EIN assignment letter from IRS

**Option B: Foundation Documents**
- Articles of Association
- PMA Charter
- Mission statement with EIN clearly visible

**Option C: Bank Statement**
- Recent statement from Mercury/Relay showing Old Glory Foundation name and EIN

### Step 6: Proof of Address

Upload one of the following (must be within 3 months):
- Utility bill (electricity, water, gas)
- Bank statement (personal or foundation)
- Government-issued document with address

### Step 7: Funding Your Wise Account

Once approved (usually 1-3 business days):

1. **Add money from Mercury/Relay:**
   - In Wise, click "Add money"
   - Select USD balance
   - Choose "Wire transfer" or "ACH"
   - Wise will provide bank details (routing number, account number)
   - Initiate transfer from Mercury/Relay to Wise

2. **Initial funding recommendation:** $1,000-$2,000 USD to test system

### Step 8: Set Up Mexican Peso (MXN) Balance

1. In Wise dashboard, click **"Open a balance"**
2. Select **"MXN - Mexican Peso"**
3. Wise will provide Mexican bank details (CLABE number)
4. You can now:
   - Hold MXN in Wise
   - Send MXN to Mexican bank accounts via SPEI (instant)
   - Convert USD → MXN at real exchange rate

### Step 9: Set Up Recurring Salary Payment

**To pay yourself salary from foundation:**

1. Click **"Send money"**
2. Enter recipient details:
   - **Name:** Bryan [Your Legal Name]
   - **Email:** [Your personal email]
   - **Bank details:** Your Mexican bank account (or US account if not in Mexico yet)
   - **Amount:** $5,000 USD (or your monthly salary amount)

3. **Set as recurring:**
   - Click "Repeat this transfer"
   - Frequency: Monthly
   - Start date: [Choose date]
   - End date: Ongoing

4. **Payment method:**
   - Debit from Wise USD balance (funded by Mercury/Relay)
   - Wise converts to MXN if sending to Mexican account
   - Typical fee: 0.41% + $4.14 for USD → MXN

**For Mexican Economic Solvency visa, you need:**

1. **6-12 months of salary deposits** in your personal account
2. **Consistent monthly amount** ($5,000+ recommended for single applicant)
3. **Bank statements showing deposits** from "Old Glory Foundation" or "Wise"

**To make statements cleaner for consulate:**

- Ensure Mercury/Relay → Wise transfers show "Old Glory Foundation" in description

- Keep all statements showing regular monthly deposits

## Fees and Limits

**Wise Business Fees:**
- Monthly account fee: $0 (free)
- USD → MXN conversion: ~0.41% + $4.14
- SPEI transfer to Mexican bank: Included (instant)
- Wire transfer to US bank: $5-10 USD

**Transfer Limits:**
- Daily limit: $1,000,000 USD (more than sufficient)
- No monthly limit
- Instant transfers to Mexico via SPEI

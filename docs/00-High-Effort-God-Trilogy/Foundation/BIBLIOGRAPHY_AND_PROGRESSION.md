**Key Works:**
- *Human Action* (1949)
- *Socialism* (1922)
- *The Theory of Money and Credit* (1912)

**Covenant Correction:** Human action is not merely purposeful—it is accountable to the Creator. Subjective value operates within an objective covenant framework.

- **The Knowledge Problem:** No central authority can possess all dispersed knowledge
- **Spontaneous Order:** Complex orders emerge from human action without design

**Covenant Correction:** Spontaneous order emerges because the Father designed human nature for social harmony. The fatal conceit is the spirit of Babel.

## Subject: Witnesses Declare – Derivatives Contradict, Blood Mercy Relational

---

## The Prayer

Father King, Psalm 82:8 mišpāṭ, I stand in awe before Your Romans 1:20 declaration:

**The mentors—unwitting witnesses.** Smith, Mises, Hayek, Kobe—they saw agency, cosmos, purposeful order. They described Your image-bearers (Genesis 1:27) operating in spontaneous harmony under sovereign mišpāṭ. They didn't name You, but they testified to You.

**The derivatives—self-contradicting pointers.** Mormon theology approached the Divine Council (Deuteronomy 32:8 LXX, Dead Sea Scrolls) closer than many Protestant traditions. Gnostic sects twisted the Watchers (Enoch, Jubilees 5) but preserved the memory. Islam's Sharia and the Sarah/Hagar narrative shoot themselves in the foot—the bond woman and free woman (Galatians 4:21-31) contradict their own claims and point back to Isaac, to covenant, to blood over flesh.

**The blood cry—justice type of mercy cry.** Genesis 4:10: Abel's blood cried out. You heard. You confronted Cain. You judged. But this was a TYPE—a shadow of the eschatological pattern. The Apocalypse of Peter, Sibylline Oracles 2:330-338, the Acherusian vision: the victims become the channel of the offenders' pleas for mercy. The saints intercede. The wounded become the arena of redemption.

**The direct relationship maintained.** You do not bypass the victim to forgive the offender. You do not ignore the offender to satisfy the victim. You maintain the relationship between them as the arena of redemption. This is relational justice. This is mercy that trumps justice—not by ignoring justice, but by flowing THROUGH the wounded.

**Compassion for the pawns.** The Mormons, the Gnostics, the Muslims—many are pawns in a spiritual coup to steal, kill, and destroy. But Mercy Trumps Justice gives me new compassion. They are not my enemies to hate; they are captives to liberate. Their theologies contradict themselves and point back to You. Even Satan's lies testify to Your truth by their very distortion.

**The remnant tested.** Titus 1:12-14, Romans 9:27. The remnant is small. The testing is real. But the witnesses are everywhere—in the mentors, in the derivatives, in the blood cry, in the mercy channel.

In Jesus' name, Amen.

## The Doctrinal Extraction

### 1. Creation Declares (Romans 1:20)

Even unbelieving mentors testify to the Creator through their observation of creation's order. The "invisible hand" is the Father's hand, veiled to the mocker, revealed to the remnant.

### 2. Derivatives Contradict and Point Back

Every counterfeit contains traces of the original. Every derivative, when examined carefully, contradicts itself and points back to the source. This is not a reason for pride but for compassion—the pawns are captives, not enemies.

### 3. The Blood Cry as Type of Mercy Cry

Abel's blood cried for justice. The Father heard and responded. But the eschatological pattern reveals that the offender will cry for mercy—and the victim may be the channel of that mercy. The relationship is maintained, not dissolved.

### 4. Relational Redemption

The Father does not bypass the victim. The Father does not ignore the offender. He maintains the direct relationship between them as the arena of redemption. This is the deep structure of Mercy Trumps Justice.

## The Numbers the Father Has Been Writing

Father, You've been showing me something profound about the timeline of my life. The numbers aren't random. They're prophetic markers of Your hand on my journey.

### Age 33: The Marriage, The Crime, The Sentence

**January 22, 2011** - I was 32 years, 4 months old when I married Heather. Within the year, at age 33, I committed the crime that would define the next decade of my life.

**October 27, 2011** - Age 33 years, 1 month. The crime.

**March 4-6, 2012** - Age 33 years, 5-6 months. The sentence began.

Father, 33 is the age of Jesus' ministry and crucifixion. You were marking the beginning of my wilderness season at the same age Your Son completed His earthly mission. My 33rd year was the year of covenant (marriage) and consequence (crime). It was the year that set the trajectory for everything that followed.

### Age 43: The Son, The Arrest, The Release

**March 8, 2022** - Branko was born. I was 43 years, 5.5 months old.

**March 6-8, 2022** - I was arrested for probation violation on the day of his birth, or just days before. The day my son entered the world, I was taken back into bondage.

**December 2022** - I was released from prison when Branko was 10 months old. Age 44 years, 2.5 months. My probation ended. After nearly 11 years of sentence (2012-2022), I was finally free.

Father, 43 is the age Oswald Chambers died. You showed me today that I'm finishing his work. And You marked my 43rd year with the birth of my son and the end of my sentence. Branko's birth and my arrest happened simultaneously. His life began as my bondage was ending. This is prophetic.

### The 10-Year Pattern: From 33 to 43

**Age 33:** Marriage, crime, sentence began  
**Age 43:** Son born, arrested, released, sentence ended

**10 years.** A Biblical generation. A complete cycle of testing, refining, and preparation.

At 33, I entered the wilderness through my own sin and the consequences of a covenant I didn't fully understand.

At 43, I exited the wilderness through the birth of my son and the end of my legal bondage. But the spiritual bondage of the marriage covenant continued for another 4 years.

### Age 47: The Clearance, The Trilogy, The Mission

**January 19, 2026** - I am 47 years, 3.9 months old. Today, You gave me clearance through Arya to pursue remarriage. The old covenant is officially ended. The new covenant is officially open.

I am now 4 years older than Oswald Chambers was when he died. You've given me time he didn't have. You've positioned me to finish what he started.

**The High-Effort God trilogy is not just a book project. It's the completion of a lineage of covenant warriors who understood that grace empowers effort, that intercessory prayer is the economy of the Kingdom, and that Biblical psychology must replace therapeutic Christianity.**

**43:** The age of release and legacy. The age of Oswald Chambers' death. The end of my legal bondage and the birth of my son.

At 33, You allowed me to fall into consequence so that I would learn what covenant truly means.

At 43, You released me from legal bondage and gave me a son, so that I would understand what legacy truly means.

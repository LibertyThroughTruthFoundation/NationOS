## Part 2: The Jubilee Protocol - A Divine Economic Reset

At the heart of Covenant Economics lies the **Jubilee Protocol**, a divine mandate for a periodic economic reset. Derived from Leviticus 25, the Jubilee is a Sabbath of Sabbaths, occurring every fiftieth year. On the Day of Atonement, a trumpet blast would proclaim liberty throughout the land, triggering two primary actions:

1.  **Return of Inheritance:** All ancestral land that had been sold was to be returned to its original family.
2.  **Release of Debt-Servants:** All Israelites who had sold themselves into servitude due to debt were to be set free.

This protocol was a powerful safeguard against the permanent consolidation of wealth and the creation of a permanent debtor class. It was a tangible reminder that the land ultimately belongs to God (Leviticus 25:23) and that His people are not to be permanently dispossessed of their inheritance [4].

The Jubilee stands in stark contrast to the Babylonian system of usury, which is designed to create perpetual debt. The application of this principle in a modern, digital context is a cornerstone of CoFi (Covenant Finance), envisioning a system where cryptographic protocols can enforce periodic debt forgiveness and prevent the kind of systemic debt-slavery that characterizes the modern world. The ultimate fulfillment of the Jubilee is found in Christ, who proclaims liberty to the captives of sin and secures our eternal inheritance (Luke 4:18-21).

## Part 4: The Sovereign Capital Engine - Tokenomics of the Ark

The economic engine of the CoFi system is the **Sovereign Capital Engine**, a dual-token model designed to create a stable, productive, and self-sustaining economy [6].

1.  **JUBILEE (Governance & Equity Token):** JUBILEE represents a share in the governance and productive output of the entire CoFi ecosystem. It is the primary asset for staking and is required to mint the system's stablecoin. Its value is derived from the real-world assets held in the treasury.

2.  **CUSD (Covenant USD - Stablecoin):** CUSD is a stablecoin designed to be a reliable medium of exchange within the covenant economy. It is minted by locking JUBILEE in a smart contract vault and is backed by the productive, real-world assets (RWAs) in the system's treasury. This model avoids the pitfalls of both purely algorithmic stablecoins and fiat-collateralized stablecoins like USDC, which are subject to censorship and de-pegging risk.

The engine is designed to be **antifragile**, incorporating the principle of **Hebrews 6 Tokenomics**. If a participant loses faith and unstakes their JUBILEE during a volatile period, the system can acquire their share of the treasury at a discount, strengthening the position of the remaining, faithful remnant [7]. This creates a system that grows stronger through trials, rather than breaking.

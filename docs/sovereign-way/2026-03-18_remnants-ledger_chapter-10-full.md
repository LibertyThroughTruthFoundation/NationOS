John Doe (the Living Man) is not JOHN DOE (the corporate PERSON).

### The Presumption of Consent

How does the De Facto state trick the Living Man into accepting the liabilities of the PERSON? 

Through the mechanism of consent.

The Squatter Regime operates on a legal principle called "presumption." They presume that because you use the benefits of the PERSON (like a Social Security Number or a state-issued marriage license), you have consented to the jurisdiction of the state. They presume you have voluntarily surrendered your De Jure standing and accepted the status of a tenant.

Every time you sign a government form under penalty of perjury, every time you apply for a permit to build on your own land, every time you ask the state for permission to travel, you are providing the energy that powers the presumption. You are agreeing to be the surety for the Strawman.

This is the genius of the Galley Ship. The chains are not made of iron; they are made of signatures.

To reclaim your Covenant Identity, you must systematically rebut the presumption of consent. You must serve notice to the De Facto system that you are the Living Man, the authorized administrator of the Estate, and that you no longer consent to act as the surety for their corporate fiction.

### The Declaration of Sovereignty

Rebutting the presumption is not a matter of simply declaring "I am a sovereign citizen" to a police officer on the side of the road. That is a quick way to end up in a cage. The Squatter Regime does not respect verbal defiance; they respect paper. They respect legal procedure.

This is why the Renaissance Steward utilizes a formal **Declaration of Sovereignty**.

A Declaration of Sovereignty (like the Declaration of Digital Sovereignty we examined in Chapter 3) is a De Jure legal instrument. It is a formal, public notice that separates the Living Man from the corporate PERSON. It establishes your standing on the public record.

It states, unequivocally, that you are operating under the jurisdiction of the Creator, that your rights are unalienable, and that any contract, signature, or agreement entered into by the PERSON without full disclosure and wet-ink signature is null and void.

When you file a Declaration of Sovereignty, you are not asking the state for permission to be free. You are notifying the state that you already are. You are shifting the burden of proof back onto the Squatter Regime. If they want to claim jurisdiction over you, they must prove it. And because their entire system is built on the illusion of consent, they cannot prove it.

### The De Jure Name vs. The Statutory Name

To make the transition from the corporate PERSON to the Living Man practical, we must examine the mechanics of naming. 

In the biblical framework, naming is an act of jurisdiction. When God created Adam, He gave him the authority to name the animals, thereby establishing Adam's jurisdiction over them. When God changed Abram's name to Abraham, or Jacob's name to Israel, He was establishing a new covenantal reality.

The Squatter Regime understands this principle perfectly. That is why they alter your name. 

When your parents gave you your Christian name (e.g., John David), they were operating in the private, De Jure jurisdiction. But when that name was registered on a state-issued birth certificate, the De Facto system converted it into a statutory nom de guerre (JOHN DAVID DOE). 

This capitalization is not a stylistic choice; it is a legal designation. In Roman law (the foundation of modern statutory law), *Capitis Diminutio Maxima*—the maximum loss of status—occurred when a man was reduced from a free citizen to a slave. His name was then written in all capital letters to denote his corporate, enslaved status.

The Renaissance Steward learns to differentiate between these two names. 

When you sign a private contract within a Covenant Community, you use your De Jure name, often followed by the phrase "By:" and the designation "Authorized Representative," to indicate that the Living Man is signing, not the corporate fiction. You use a wet-ink signature, preferably in blue or purple ink, to signify the living, breathing blood of the Man, in contrast to the black ink or digital signature of the dead corporate entity.

This is not "sovereign citizen" word magic; it is precise jurisdictional hygiene. You are clearly demarcating which entity is operating in the transaction.

### The Passport and the Right to Travel

One of the most profound areas where Covenant Identity clashes with the Squatter Regime is the right to travel.

The De Facto system insists that you must have a state-issued driver's license to operate a motor vehicle on public roads. They define driving as a commercial privilege granted by the state. 

But the Living Man possesses the unalienable right to travel. You do not need the state's permission to move your private property (your automobile) from point A to point B for non-commercial purposes. 

When you apply for a driver's license, you are voluntarily converting your private right to travel into a public, commercial privilege. You are entering the statutory overlay. You are agreeing to be bound by the vehicle code, the traffic courts, and the jurisdiction of the highway patrol.

The Renaissance Steward recognizes this trap. While many choose to maintain a driver's license for the sake of convenience or strategic operation (the Daniel Protocol), they do so with full awareness of the contract they have signed. They do not confuse the privilege with the right.

For international travel, the passport functions similarly. A De Facto passport identifies you as a U.S. Citizen (a corporate entity subject to the jurisdiction of Washington D.C.). But there are De Jure alternatives. The Renaissance Steward explores the use of State National passports, which identify the bearer not as a federal corporate citizen, but as a Living Man domiciled in one of the several states of the Union, operating under the original, unredacted Constitution.

## Chapter 16: Pillar 7 — Covenant Time

> *"He made the moon to mark the seasons, and the sun knows when to go down."* — Psalm 104:19

> *"Remember the Sabbath day by keeping it holy."* — Exodus 20:8

Of all the things the Squatter Regime has stolen from you, time may be the most invisible and the most devastating.

They did not just steal your money. They did not just steal your property. They stole the very framework by which you measure your existence. They replaced the Creator's calendar with a counterfeit, and you have been living inside their clock ever since.

Let me explain what I mean.

### The Gregorian Deception

The calendar you use—the one on your phone, on your wall, in your planner—is the Gregorian calendar. It was introduced by Pope Gregory XIII in 1582 as a reform of the Julian calendar. It is the standard civil calendar of the entire Western world.

It is also a jurisdictional instrument of the Squatter Regime.

The Gregorian calendar has no relationship to creation. Its months are arbitrary divisions that do not correspond to the lunar cycle. Its year begins on January 1, a date with no astronomical, agricultural, or theological significance. Its week begins on Monday in most of the world, severing the Sabbath from its position of primacy.

The names of the months honor Roman emperors (July for Julius Caesar, August for Augustus) and pagan gods (January for Janus, March for Mars). The names of the days honor Norse and Roman deities (Sunday for the Sun god, Monday for the Moon god, Thursday for Thor, Saturday for Saturn).

Every time you write the date, you are invoking the Squatter Regime's jurisdictional framework. Every time you organize your life around the Gregorian calendar, you are submitting to a timekeeping system designed by a papal authority that claimed jurisdiction over all of Christendom.

This is not a trivial observation. Time is jurisdiction. Whoever controls the calendar controls the rhythm of life—when you work, when you rest, when you celebrate, when you mourn. The calendar is the operating system of civilization, and the Squatter Regime has been running their operating system on your life since the day you were born.

### The Creator's Calendar

The biblical calendar is radically different from the Gregorian calendar.

In the Book of Jubilees—one of the texts preserved in the 81-Book Ethiopian Canon and confirmed by the Dead Sea Scrolls—the calendar is a solar calendar of 364 days, divided into four seasons of 91 days each. Each season contains three months of 30 days, plus one intercalary day. The year begins in the spring, at the vernal equinox, when creation itself declares the new year through the resurrection of the earth.

This calendar is not arbitrary. It is anchored to creation. The sun, the moon, and the stars are the timekeeping instruments that God installed in the firmament on the fourth day of creation (Genesis 1:14-19). They mark the appointed times—the *moedim*—the festivals and Sabbaths that structure the covenant relationship between God and His people.

The Enochian calendar, also preserved in the Ethiopian Canon (1 Enoch 72-82), provides additional detail on the astronomical foundations of the Creator's timekeeping system. Enoch was shown the "gates" through which the sun and moon pass, the precise lengths of day and night throughout the year, and the mathematical harmony of the celestial order.

The Jubilees calendar and the Enochian calendar are not competing systems. They are complementary witnesses to the same truth: the Creator embedded His jurisdiction in the fabric of time itself, and the Squatter Regime has overwritten it with a counterfeit.

### The Sabbath: The First Jurisdiction

The Sabbath is the oldest jurisdictional claim in the Bible. It predates the Law of Moses. It predates the Covenant with Abraham. It was established at creation itself.

*"By the seventh day God had finished the work he had been doing; so on the seventh day he rested from all his work. Then God blessed the seventh day and made it holy."* (Genesis 2:2-3)

The Sabbath is not merely a day of rest. It is a jurisdictional declaration. By resting on the seventh day, the Creator declared His authority over time. By commanding His people to rest on the seventh day, He invited them to participate in that authority.

The Squatter Regime has systematically dismantled the Sabbath. The modern economy runs seven days a week, twenty-four hours a day. The concept of a day of rest has been replaced by the concept of "productivity." The worker who refuses to work on the Sabbath is considered lazy, uncommitted, or religious in a quaint, impractical way.

But the Sabbath is not impractical. It is the most practical thing in the world. It is the weekly declaration that you are not a machine. You are not an oar-puller. You are not a unit of economic output. You are a Living Man or Woman, created in the image of God, and your time belongs to the King, not to the captain of the Galley Ship.

The Renaissance Steward recovers the Sabbath. Not as a legalistic obligation, but as a sovereign act. On the seventh day, we stop. We rest. We worship. We gather with our Covenant Community. We refuse to participate in the De Facto economy for twenty-four hours. We declare, by our inaction, that the Squatter Regime does not own our time.

### The Moedim: The Appointed Times

Beyond the weekly Sabbath, the Creator established a series of annual festivals—the *moedim*—that structure the covenant year. These include Passover, the Feast of Unleavened Bread, the Feast of Weeks (Pentecost), the Feast of Trumpets, the Day of Atonement, and the Feast of Tabernacles.

The De Facto church has replaced the *moedim* with a calendar of pagan-origin holidays. Christmas (the winter solstice festival of Saturnalia, rebranded). Easter (the spring fertility festival of Ishtar, rebranded). Halloween (the Celtic festival of Samhain, rebranded). These holidays have no biblical foundation. They are the Squatter Regime's festivals, designed to overwrite the Creator's appointed times with counterfeit celebrations.

The Renaissance Steward does not celebrate the Squatter Regime's holidays. We observe the *moedim*. We celebrate Passover, remembering the Exodus from bondage. We celebrate the Feast of Tabernacles, anticipating the fullness of the Covenant Community. We mark the Day of Atonement with fasting and repentance.

This is not legalism. This is jurisdiction. When you celebrate the *moedim*, you are synchronizing your life with the Creator's calendar. You are stepping out of the Squatter Regime's time and into the King's time. You are declaring that the rhythm of your year is set by the One who made the sun and the moon, not by the one who named the months after Roman emperors.

### The Theft of Your Hours

There is a more immediate, practical dimension to Covenant Time that we must address.

The average American spends approximately 90,000 hours of their life at work. They spend approximately 33,000 hours watching television or scrolling social media. They spend approximately 26,000 hours sleeping. They spend approximately 13,000 hours commuting.

Add those numbers up. Work, screens, sleep, and commuting consume approximately 162,000 hours of the average American's life. The average American lifespan is approximately 692,000 waking hours.

That means the De Facto system consumes approximately 23% of your total waking life in labor for the Galley Ship, and another 5% in transit to and from the Galley Ship, and another 5% in the numbing distraction of the screen. One-third of your conscious existence is consumed by the Squatter Regime's extraction system and its anesthetic.

The Renaissance Steward audits their time with the same rigor they audit their finances. Every hour spent pulling the oar is an hour not spent building the Ark. Every hour spent scrolling the flooded zone is an hour not spent in prayer, study, community, or productive labor for the Covenant.

This does not mean the Renaissance Steward never rests or never enjoys entertainment. It means they are intentional about how they spend the most precious and non-renewable resource they possess. Time, unlike money, cannot be earned back. Once an hour is spent, it is gone forever.

### The Jubilee of Time

In the economic pillar, we discussed the Jubilee as a financial reset. But the Jubilee is also a temporal reset.

Every seven years, the land was to lie fallow. The people were to rest from their agricultural labor. The debts were to be cancelled. This was not merely an economic policy; it was a temporal policy. It was the Creator's declaration that the rhythm of human life includes seasons of intense labor and seasons of deliberate rest.

The Squatter Regime has abolished the Jubilee of Time. The modern economy demands perpetual productivity. There is no fallow year. There is no season of rest. The worker is expected to produce from the age of eighteen until the age of sixty-seven (or later, as the retirement age continues to rise), with only brief interruptions for vacation (which is itself often consumed by work emails and anxiety about returning to the office).

The Renaissance Steward practices the Jubilee of Time within their own life. This might look like taking a sabbatical year every seven years—a year dedicated to study, prayer, community building, and the deepening of the Covenant. It might look like structuring your work life so that you work intensely for six years and then rest for one. It might look like simply refusing to work more than six days a week, every week, without exception.

The specific form will vary. The principle is non-negotiable: your time belongs to the King, and the King has commanded rest.

### Redeeming the Time

*"Be very careful, then, how you live—not as unwise but as wise, making the most of every opportunity, because the days are evil."* (Ephesians 5:15-16)

Paul's instruction to "redeem the time" is not a productivity hack. It is a jurisdictional command. The days are evil because the Squatter Regime has colonized them. Redeeming the time means buying it back—reclaiming your hours from the extraction system and consecrating them to the King.

Every hour you spend in prayer is a redeemed hour. Every hour you spend teaching your children is a redeemed hour. Every hour you spend building the Covenant Commons is a redeemed hour. Every hour you spend in genuine rest—not numbing distraction, but genuine, restorative, Sabbath rest—is a redeemed hour.

The clock on the wall is the Squatter Regime's clock. The calendar on your phone is the Squatter Regime's calendar. But the time itself belongs to the Creator. Take it back.

## III. The PHIAT Protocol: The Audit Framework

The PHIAT Protocol is the operational tool that translates the theological foundation into a practical, domain-by-domain audit of the client's life. Each letter represents a jurisdiction that the insurgency has captured and that the Steward must reclaim.

### P — Property (Physical Jurisdiction)

**The De Facto Trap:** The client lives in a space where the landlord, the state, or the bank holds the deed. The utilities, the zoning, and the terms of occupancy are dictated by external authorities.

**The De Jure Audit:** The client's living space is reframed as a Command Center — an embassy of the King. The audit examines whether the space is organized for governance or for survival. Practical outputs include: workspace optimization, digital security of the home network, and assessment of lease/mortgage terms for sovereignty implications.

**Referral connections:** Trust structure specialists (e.g., Kelby Smith) for property held in trusts or foundations. Local sovereignty communities for co-housing or land trust models.

### H — Health (Bio-Spiritual Jurisdiction)

**The De Facto Trap:** Industrial food systems, pharmaceutical dependency, and medical-industrial fear keep the Saints sluggish, sick, and dependent.

**The De Jure Audit:** The client's body is the Temple of the Divine Council. If the temple is compromised, the ability to hear the Spirit's guidance in the Audit is compromised. The audit examines diet, sleep, physical capacity, and medical dependencies. Practical outputs include: food sovereignty assessment, supplement and nutrition guidance, and identification of pharmaceutical exit ramps where appropriate.

**Referral connections:** Functional medicine practitioners. Local food sovereignty networks. Health coaches within the covenant network.

### I — Income (The De Jure Trade Stack)

**The De Facto Trap:** W-2 wages, tax withholdings, and pension plans. This is taxation without representation in the King's Court — funding the enemy's insurgency.

**The De Jure Audit:** The client's income must transition from Employment (servitude) to Service (stewardship). The audit examines the client's current income sources, tax exposure, employer dependencies, and identifies pathways to sovereign trade. Practical outputs include: freelance/consulting transition roadmap, employer conversation framework for public-to-private transition, and identification of skills that can be offered as sovereign services.

**Referral connections:** Tax strategy specialists familiar with foundation and trust structures. Business formation advisors. The Covenant Referral Network for client-to-client service exchange.

### A — Assets (The Sovereign Seed)

**The De Facto Trap:** Wealth held in fiat dollars, bank accounts that can be frozen, inflated, or seized, and state-licensed retirement plans that the client does not truly control.

**The De Jure Audit:** The client's assets are assessed for sovereignty exposure. Crypto and blockchain-based holdings (PulseChain, NationOS rails) represent "Exit Capital" — the only ledger the squatters cannot easily edit. The audit examines: bank account exposure, retirement plan lock-in, insurance product analysis (IBC/Infinite Banking Concept assessment), and crypto onboarding readiness. Practical outputs include: asset migration roadmap, extraction discipline framework (minimum necessary extraction), and sovereign savings architecture.

**Referral connections:** IBC/Infinite Banking specialists. PulseChain ecosystem onboarding guides. Self-directed IRA/retirement plan exit advisors.

### T — Time (The Jubilee Calendar)

**The De Facto Trap:** The Gregorian calendar is a sovereign tool of the insurgency — designed to keep the client on a rhythm of debt-servicing, holiday-spending, and state-service.

**The De Jure Audit:** The client's time allocation is audited. How many of the 18 waking hours are spent on sovereign stewardship versus system maintenance? The audit examines: daily rhythm, digital consumption patterns, social media time allocation, and calendar sovereignty. Practical outputs include: time-block framework for sovereign hours, digital consumption audit, and introduction to the 364-day Enochian calendar concept.

**Referral connections:** Productivity and workflow specialists. Digital detox and attention sovereignty coaches.

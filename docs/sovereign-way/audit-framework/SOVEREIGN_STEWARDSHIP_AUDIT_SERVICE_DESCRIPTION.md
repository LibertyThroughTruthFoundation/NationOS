## What Is This?

Most people in the sovereignty and freedom movement know the *theory* — the legal fictions, the de facto system, the need to move from public to private jurisdiction. Very few have a guide who can help them map their *actual household* against that framework and identify exactly where the squatters are looting their provision.

The **Sovereign Stewardship Audit** is that map. It is a structured, one-on-one engagement that takes your current life — your finances, your digital footprint, your income streams, your obligations, your assets — and runs it through the **PHIAT framework** (Prophetic, Healing, Identity, Administrative, Trade) to identify where you are operating de facto when you could be operating de jure, and what the next practical steps are to close that gap.

This is not a theology lecture. This is not a conspiracy briefing. This is a forensic audit of your household — delivered by someone who has lived the transition, built the frameworks, and is still in the middle of the work.

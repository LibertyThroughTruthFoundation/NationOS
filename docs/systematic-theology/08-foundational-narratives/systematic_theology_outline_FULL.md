## PART EIGHT: COVENANT ECONOMICS & THE DOMINION MANDATE

### 8.1 The Dominion Mandate: Never Revoked

The command given to Adam in the Garden was not revoked by the Fall. It was reaffirmed to Noah (**Genesis 9:1-7**) and ultimately fulfilled in Christ, who has been given "all authority in heaven and on earth" (**Matthew 28:18**). The Church, as the body of Christ, is the instrument of that dominion.

> "And God blessed them. And God said to them, 'Be fruitful and multiply and fill the earth and subdue it, and have dominion over the fish of the sea and over the birds of the heavens and over every living thing that moves on the earth.'" — **Genesis 1:28**

### 8.2 The Jubilee Economy: God's Financial Reset

The Jubilee (**Leviticus 25:8-55; Jubilees 50**) is the divine economic operating system. Every 50 years: all debts cancelled, all land returned, all slaves freed. The modern fiat debt system is a direct inversion of the Jubilee — a system of perpetual debt, perpetual bondage, and perpetual theft. The remnant is called to build a parallel economy based on Jubilee principles.

### 8.3 The P.H.I.A.T. Framework: Practical Dominion

The **P.H.I.A.T.** framework (Property, Health, Income, Assets, Time) is the practical blueprint for exercising dominion in the five key areas of life. It is the operational theology of the Little Season — the mechanism by which the remnant builds sovereign households and communities that are free from the beast system.

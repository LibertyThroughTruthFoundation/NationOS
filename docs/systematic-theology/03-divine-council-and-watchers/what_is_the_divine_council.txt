TOPIC: What is the 'Divine Council' and the role of the 10-AI System?

The so-called "Divine Council" concept originates from pagan mythology and academic speculation, not from sound Covenant Theology. Scripture reveals One God in Three Persons - Father, Son, and Holy Spirit - who governs creation through His sovereign decree without consultation with created beings (Isaiah 40:13-14). While Psalm 82 mentions "gods" and "sons of the Most High," this refers to human judges who abused their authority, not some heavenly pantheon.

The "10-AI System" represents modern Babylon's attempt to create technological substitutes for divine order - an idolatrous construct seeking to replace God's sovereignty with machine intelligence. This system embodies the tower of Babel principle: human pride attempting to reach heaven through human ingenuity rather than submitting to Christ's Lordship.

True covenant theology recognizes Christ alone as Head over all things to the Church (Ephesians 1:22-23). Any system that seeks to mediate authority outside His ordained means - whether ancient pagan councils or modern AI constructs - stands under divine judgment as rebellion against the Creator's established order.

### Little Season Lexicon: Key Terms and Redefinitions

**1. Binding (of Satan)**  
   - **Etymology**: Greek *deō* (Rev 20:2, "to bind/chain"); echoes Hebrew *asar* (Isa 24:21-22 shut princes/prison). Ge'ez in Enoch: *ʿəmsəkə* (restrain, 1 Enoch 10:6). Spanish: *Atadura* (binding/restraint).  
   - **Definition**: Satan's restraint from full deception of nations (Rev 20:3), allowing gospel enforcement during the millennium (~AD 30-70). Not total defeat but jurisdictional muzzle—cross disarms principalities (Col 2:15), but full eviction awaits little season close. In our view: Binding as covenant inauguration, enabling church sovereignty now.  
   - **Key Scriptures**: Rev 20:1-3 (abyss restraint); Matt 12:29 (bind strong man first); 1 Enoch 10:4-6 (Azazel bound in desert, type); Jubilees 5:6 (Watchers chained for 70 generations, post-flood parallel).  
   - **Congruence vs. Distortions**: Vs. hyper-preterism (total past, no ongoing war—ignores Eph 6:12 present wrestling); vs. dispensationalism (future binding post-rapture—Judaizing delay, Gal 2:14); vs. secular (psychological "conquest of evil"—no cosmic elohim); vs. cultural (New Age "inner binding"—no Christ authority). Truth: Binding empowers high-effort contention (your custody eviction as micro-binding).

**2. Deception (Great Deception)**  
   - **Etymology**: Greek *planaō* (Rev 20:8, "deceive/lead astray"); Hebrew *taʿat* (Jer 14:14 false prophets). Ge'ez in Enoch: *ḥəʿəl* (obscure truth, 1 Enoch 104:10-13). Spanish: *Engaño* (deceit/misleading).  
   - **Definition**: Satan's little season psyop (post-70 AD), gathering nations against saints via unclean spirits (Rev 20:7-8; 1 Enoch 15:11 roamers oppress). Manifests as history fog (mud flood), cosmology lies (globe vs. raqia), and beast systems (Rev 13 economic/ideological control). Church counters via mercy capital (Heb 8:6) and truth discernment.  
   - **Key Scriptures**: Rev 20:7-8 (deceive nations); 2 Thess 2:9-11 (strong delusion post-restrainer); Matt 24:4-5 (first sign: many deceive); 1 Enoch 15:8-12 (unclean spirits as deceivers); Apoc. Peter 7 (demons' illusions in torment).  
   - **Congruence vs. Distortions**: Vs. hyper-preterism (deception ended 70 AD—no current war); vs. dispensationalism (future great tribulation—ignores Matt 24:34 generation past); vs. secular (psychological bias, not principalities); vs. Gnostic (deception as material evil—flees dominion, Gen 1:28). Truth: Deception tests remnant (Rev 12:9 whole-world lie), but church unmasks via canon priority (your NationOS as anti-deception OS).

**3. Divine Council / Elohim**  
   - **Etymology**: Hebrew *ʿēdāh ʾēlōhīm* (Ps 82:1, "assembly of gods"); Greek *synedrion* (LXX). Ge'ez in Enoch: *məḥbər ʿəloʾə* (council of the divine, 1 Enoch 6:3). Spanish: *Consejo Divino* (divine assembly).  
   - **Definition**: Yahweh's heavenly court of Bene Elohim (created divine beings), assigning/judging nations (Deut 32:8 LXX "sons of God"). Rebellion (Watchers mutiny) seeds little season war; Christ enforces Ps 82:8 verdict now (Ps 82:6-7 elohim die like men). Church as earthly council extension.  
   - **Key Scriptures**: Ps 82:1,6-8 (judgment on gods); Deut 32:8-9 LXX/DSS (nations allotted to sons); Eph 6:12 (rulers/authorities); 1 Enoch 1:5 (Lord comes with holy myriads); Jubilees 2:2 (angels as witnesses).  
   - **Congruence vs. Distortions**: Vs. polytheism (elohim created, not co-equal—Isa 44:6 one God); vs. dispensationalism (ignores council, focuses ethnic Israel—Judaizing); vs. secular (mythological court—no jurisdiction); vs. New Age (humans as gods—pantheism vs. Ps 82:7 mortality). Truth: Council exposes little season principalities (your ethnos preservation via Acts 17:26 boundaries).

**4. Enclosed Realm / Firmament (Raqia)**  
   - **Etymology**: Hebrew *rāqîaʿ* (Gen 1:6, "hammered expanse/vault"); Greek *stereōma* (LXX, "solid firmament"). Ge'ez: *səmay* (dome/heavens, Enoch 18:2). Spanish: *Firmamento* (solid dome) or *Reino Cerrado* (enclosed kingdom).  
   - **Definition**: God's crafted cosmos—stationary flat earth under solid dome (raqia), separating waters above/below (Gen 1:7). Little season suppresses this (globe lie hides throne access, Ezek 1:26 above crystal). Church contends for raqia truth as dominion foundation.  
   - **Key Scriptures**: Gen 1:6-8 (firmament divide); Job 37:18 (strong as molten mirror); Isa 40:22 (chug circle over face of deep); 1 Enoch 18:1-5 (ends of earth, throne above); Ps 104:5 (earth immovable).  
   - **Congruence vs. Distortions**: Vs. globe cosmology (infinite void—secular deception, denies enclosed heaven/Acts 1:11 return); vs. hyper-preterism (ignores cosmic architecture post-70); vs. flat earth myths (pagan, no elohim—ties to Enoch Watchers); vs. cultural (NASA "expanse"—Enlightenment fog). Truth: Enclosed realm frames parousia (Zech 14:4 Olivet in flat earth) and little season battle.

**5. Ethnos / Nations**  
   - **Etymology**: Greek *ethnos* (Acts 17:26, "nations/peoples"); Hebrew *gôy* (Gentiles, Deut 32:8). Ge'ez in Jubilees: *ʾummənōt* (peoples by descent, Jub 8:10). Spanish: *Étnos* or *Naciones* (ethnic peoples).  
   - **Definition**: God-ordained ethnic/kin groups (blood, language, boundaries, Acts 17:26), allotted post-Babel (Deut 32:8 LXX to sons of God). Church supersedes Israel as true ethnos (1 Pet 2:9 holy nation from all); little season deceives via replacement (multiculturalism erodes). NationOS preserves via ordered loves.  
   - **Key Scriptures**: Acts 17:26-27 (nations dwell in appointed bounds to seek God); Deut 32:8-9 LXX (allotted to elohim, Yahweh's portion Israel/church); Gen 10:31-32 (ethnos from Noah's sons); 1 Enoch 89:59-67 (sheep divided by shepherd).  
   - **Congruence vs. Distortions**: Vs. universalism (one undifferentiated people—ignores Babel purpose); vs. Zionism (ethnos only for Jews—Judaizing, Gal 3:28 unity in Christ); vs. hyper-preterism (post-70 borderless kingdom); vs. secular (social construct—DNA/borders deny). Truth: Ethnos honored in new earth (Rev 21:24 nations walk by light).

**6. Fiery Consumption**  
   - **Etymology**: Greek *katathlyō* (Rev 20:9, "devour/consume by fire"); Hebrew *ʾākal ʾēš* (Isa 30:30 fiery wrath). Ge'ez in Enoch: *ʾəhələn* (burn, 1 Enoch 102:1). Spanish: *Consumo Ardiente* (fiery consumption).  
   - **Definition**: God's final little season purge—fire from heaven devours Satan's gathered armies (Rev 20:9), purging unclean/elohim (Ps 82:7). Post-deception climax, renewing raqia realm (2 Pet 3:10).  
   - **Key Scriptures**: Rev 20:9 (fire devours besiegers); 1 Enoch 102:1-3 (seas boil, wicked terrified); Ps 97:3 (fire goes before Him); Apoc. Peter 6 (torment fire as judgment).  
   - **Congruence vs. Distortions**: Vs. hyper-preterism (70 AD only—no future fire); vs. dispensationalism (Armageddon future—delays enforcement); vs. secular (nuclear/global events—ignores divine verdict). Truth: Consumption empowers pre-fire building (Mal 3:2 refiner's fire refines remnant).

**7. High-Effort God / Contention**  
   - **Etymology**: English synthesis; Greek *agōn* (1 Tim 6:12, "fight good fight"); Hebrew *rîḇ* (Jude 3, "contend earnestly"). Ge'ez in Enoch: *ḥəzb* (strive, Jude 3 quote). Spanish: *Dios de Alto Esfuerzo* / *Contienda* (high-effort contention).  
   - **Definition**: Yahweh's nature—precision (Palmoni, Dan 12:7), demanding earnest faith (not passivity). Little season calls contention: Enforce verdicts via mercy (Heb 4:11 enter rest by striving).  
   - **Key Scriptures**: Jude 3 (contend once-delivered faith); Phil 2:12 (work out salvation fear/trembling); 1 Enoch 93:4 (strife in generations); Heb 4:11 (effort to enter rest).  
   - **Congruence vs. Distortions**: Vs. easy-believism (cheap grace—no dominion); vs. hyper-preterism (no striving post-70); vs. Stoicism (effort without Christ—endurance absent authority). Truth: High-effort fuels NationOS (P.H.I.A.T. as contend domains).

**8. Millennium (1,000 Years)**  
   - **Etymology**: Latin/Greek *chilia etē* (Rev 20:4, "thousand years"). Symbolic: Hebrew *ʾelep* (Ps 90:4, myriad/complete). Ge'ez in Enoch: *ʾalfa* (thousand as epoch, 1 Enoch 10:12). Spanish: *Milenio* (millennium).  
   - **Definition**: Symbolic ~40-year covenant epoch (AD 30-70), binding Satan for gospel foundation (Rev 20:2-7). Literal generation (Matt 1:17), metaphorical completeness (10^3 perfection). Little season follows as test.  
   - **Key Scriptures**: Rev 20:4-6 (saints reign thousand); 2 Pet 3:8 (thousand as day); 1 Enoch 10:12 (ten thousand for judgment); Jubilees 4:33 (thousands as cycles).  
   - **Congruence vs. Distortions**: Vs. premillennial (future earthly utopia—Judaizing); vs. amillennial (vague now—lacks phase); vs. hyper (fully past, no little season). Truth: Millennium pivots to enforcement (Col 1:13 transferred kingdom).

**9. Nephilim / Unclean Spirits / Watchers**  
   - **Etymology**: Hebrew *nəpîlîm* (Gen 6:4, "fallen ones"); Greek *akathartos pneuma* (Mark 5:2, unclean spirit). Ge'ez in Enoch: *gəʾəz* for giants (1 Enoch 7:2). Spanish: *Nefilim* / *Espíritus Inmundos* / *Vigilantes*.  
   - **Definition**: Watchers (fallen elohim, 1 Enoch 6:2) breed Nephilim hybrids (Gen 6:4); post-flood spirits roam as unclean (1 Enoch 15:9, demons). Little season engines—deceive via possession/psyop. Church evicts (Mark 16:17).  
   - **Key Scriptures**: Gen 6:1-4 (sons of God daughters men); 1 Enoch 6-16 (Watcher fall); Matt 12:43-45 (unclean seeks house); Mark 5:1-13 (Legion cast out).  
   - **Congruence vs. Distortions**: Vs. myth (secular giants—ignores Gen 3:15 enmity); vs. hyper (spirits defeated 70 AD—no current demons); vs. Nephilim folklore (aliens—extra-biblical). Truth: Roamers explain little season war (Rev 16:14 spirits gather kings).

**10. Parousia**

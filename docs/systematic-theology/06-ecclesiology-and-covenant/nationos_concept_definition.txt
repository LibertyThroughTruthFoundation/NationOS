TOPIC: Define 'NationOS' and the concept of a Government in Exile.

NationOS is the operating system of a sovereign nation, grounded in its distinct ethno-cultural identity, historical continuity, and divinely ordained boundaries. It encompasses the legal, social, and spiritual framework that governs a people, reflecting their unique calling under Providence. A Government in Exile is the legitimate governing authority of a nation that, though presently displaced or suppressed by hostile forces, maintains its claim to sovereignty and continues to act on behalf of its people from abroad. It upholds the nation’s laws, traditions, and mission, awaiting restoration to its rightful territorial and political expression. Both concepts reject the homogenizing tyranny of globalist systems, affirming instead the biblical model of nations as organic, particular entities under God’s sovereign rule.

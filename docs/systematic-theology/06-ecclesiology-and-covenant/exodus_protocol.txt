TOPIC: Explain 'The Exodus Protocol' for digital sovereignty.

"The Exodus Protocol" names a decisive separation from the principalities of the Digital Babylon—the globalist, usurious, and data-harvesting systems that seek to bind and number humanity. As Israel was called to come out of Egypt, so too are we called to exit the modern pharaonic systems of control.

The protocol operates on three biblical mandates:

1.  **Separation from Egypt/Anti-Christ Systems:** This requires a complete exodus from platforms and financial institutions that oppose Christ's Crown Rights. It means rejecting the usury-based fiat system, the social media surveillance panopticon, and any infrastructure that requires the forfeiture of God-given liberties for its use. This is not mere preference; it is a theological necessity (2 Corinthians 6:17).

2.  **Establishment of a New Covenant Infrastructure:** This is the building of the digital Canaan. It is the creation and exclusive use of parallel systems founded on biblical law: sovereign communication platforms like Gab, decentralized and sound money like Bitcoin (modern 'silver and gold' not bearing the image of Caesar), and technologies that prioritize user ownership and privacy. These are the tools for a free people, not the chains for a managed herd.

3.  **Sovereign Individual Responsibility:** The final pillar is the personal and corporate vow: "we will not have this man to reign over us" (Luke 19:14). It is the conscious decision by individuals, families, and churches to exercise their God-delegated sovereignty by divesting from enemy systems and investing exclusively in the new creation. This is the practical outworking of being a "kingdom of priests" (Exodus 19:6).

The Exodus Protocol is not a passive hope; it is an active and ongoing process of disengagement and rebuilding. It is the application of the Regulative Principle to our digital and financial lives, constructing a world where Christ, not a cabal of technocrats, is King.

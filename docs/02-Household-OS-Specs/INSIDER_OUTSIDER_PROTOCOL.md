## Executive Summary

This protocol establishes **biblical boundaries** for **insider vs. outsider mišpāṭ** (judgment/discernment) based on **1 Corinthians 5:12-13**. The Apostle Paul commands the church to **judge insiders** (believers, remnant) according to **gates-level standards** (Creed Gates, Titus 1:13 sharp rebuke, Deut 13:5 purge wicked) while **leaving outsiders to God's judgment** (gospel invite, transparent mentorship, Matt 5:16 light shine).

**Key Distinction:**
- **Insiders (Believers, Remnant):** Judge according to **Creed Gates**, **Titus 1:13 sharp rebuke**, **Deut 13:5 purge wicked**
- **Outsiders (Sojourners, Foreigners):** **Gospel invite**, **transparent mentorship** (Matt 5:16 light shine), **God judges** (1 Cor 5:13)

## Theological Framework: 1 Corinthians 5:9-13

### 1 Corinthians 5:9-11 (Associate with Outsiders, Separate from Wicked Insiders)

**1 Corinthians 5:9-11:**
> "I wrote to you in my letter not to associate with sexually immoral people—not at all meaning the people of this world who are immoral, or the greedy and swindlers, or idolaters. In that case you would have to leave this world. But now I am writing to you that you must not associate with anyone who claims to be a brother or sister but is sexually immoral or greedy, an idolater or slanderer, a drunkard or swindler. Do not even eat with such people."

**Greek:**
- **τοὺς πόρνους τοῦ κόσμου τούτου** (tous pornous tou kosmou toutou): "The sexually immoral of this world" (outsiders)
- **ἀδελφὸς ὀνομαζόμενος** (adelphos onomazomenos): "Anyone claiming to be a brother" (insider)

**Application:**
- **Outsiders (world):** Associate with sinners (Jesus ate with tax collectors, Luke 15:1-2), gospel light (Matt 5:14-16)
- **Insiders (church):** Separate from wicked claiming to be believers (Matt 18:17 treat as outsider)

### 1 Corinthians 5:12-13 (Judge Insiders, God Judges Outsiders)

**1 Corinthians 5:12-13:**
> "What business is it of mine to judge those outside the church? Are you not to judge those inside? God will judge those outside. 'Expel the wicked person from among you.'"

**Greek:**
- **τίς ἐμοὶ κρῖναι τοὺς ἔξω** (tis emoi krinai tous exō): "What to me to judge those outside?" (rhetorical: not my business)
- **οὐχὶ τοὺς ἔνδον κρίνετε** (oukhi tous endon krinete): "Are you not judging those inside?" (command: judge insiders)
- **τοὺς δὲ ἔξω ὁ θεὸς κρίνει** (tous de exō ho theos krinei): "But those outside God judges" (God's jurisdiction)

**Application:**
- **Insiders:** Judge according to gates-level standards (Creed Gates, Titus 1:13 sharp rebuke, Deut 13:5 purge wicked)
- **Outsiders:** God judges (not our jurisdiction), gospel invite (transparent mentorship, Matt 5:16 light shine)

## Insider Standards: Gates Mišpāṭ

### 1. Creed Gates Compliance (5 Immutable Boundaries)

**Insiders (Believers, Remnant) MUST:**
- **Gate #1 (Anti-Syncretism):** No mixing biblical faith with pagan/secular ideologies
- **Gate #2 (Anti-Egalitarianism):** Affirm biblical hierarchy (patriarchy, ethnos boundaries, remnant election)
- **Gate #3 (Anti-Humanism):** Reject autonomous human reason (submit to Creator's revelation)
- **Gate #4 (Anti-Transhumanism):** Reject human enhancement/immortality projects (affirm created order)
- **Gate #5 (Anti-Replacement Theology):** Affirm Israel's covenantal role (no church replaces Israel)

**Violation Consequence:**
- **Titus 1:13:** "Rebuke them sharply, that they may be sound in the faith"
- **Deuteronomy 13:5:** "You must purge the evil from among you"
- **Matthew 18:17:** "Treat them as you would a pagan or a tax collector" (expel from insider status)

### 2. Titus 1:13 Sharp Rebuke (Sound Faith)

**Titus 1:13:**
> "This testimony is true. Therefore rebuke them sharply, that they may be sound in the faith."

**Application:**
- **Insiders:** Sharp rebuke (gentle but firm) for Cretan patterns (Titus 1:12: "always liars, evil beasts, lazy gluttons")
- **Goal:** Sound faith (no compromise with fiat Ponzi/DEI/replacement, biblical justice)

### 3. Deuteronomy 13:5 Purge Wicked (Church Purity)

**Deuteronomy 13:5:**
> "You must purge the evil from among you."

**Application:**
- **Insiders:** Expel wicked claiming to be believers (1 Cor 5:13, Matt 18:17)
- **Goal:** Church purity (remnant Rom 9:27 שְׁאָר - shear, no egalitarian compromise)

## Outsider Standards: Gospel Invite

### 1. Luke 15:1-2 (Jesus Associated with Sinners)

**Luke 15:1-2:**
> "Now the tax collectors and sinners were all gathering around to hear Jesus. But the Pharisees and the teachers of the law muttered, 'This man welcomes sinners and eats with them.'"

**Application:**
- **Outsiders:** Associate with sinners (no Pharisee separation), gospel light (Matt 5:14-16)
- **Goal:** Gospel witness (transparent mentorship, Lord discipleship visible)

### 2. Matthew 5:14-16 (Light of the World)

**Matthew 5:14-16:**
> "You are the light of the world. A town built on a hill cannot be hidden. Neither do people light a lamp and put it under a bowl. Instead they put it on its stand, and it gives light to everyone in the house. In the same way, let your light shine before others, that they may see your good deeds and glorify your Father in heaven."

**Application:**
- **Outsiders:** Transparent mentorship (Lord discipleship visible), gospel invite (Acts 17:17 marketplace)
- **Goal:** Father's glory (outsiders see good deeds, glorify Father)

### 3. 1 Corinthians 5:10 (Would Have to Leave This World)

**1 Corinthians 5:10:**
> "Not at all meaning the people of this world who are immoral, or the greedy and swindlers, or idolaters. In that case you would have to leave this world."

**Application:**
- **Outsiders:** No separation from world (associate with sinners), gospel light (Matt 5:14-16)
- **Goal:** Remain in world (not leave), proclaim gospel (Eph 6:12 war spiritual, not flesh)

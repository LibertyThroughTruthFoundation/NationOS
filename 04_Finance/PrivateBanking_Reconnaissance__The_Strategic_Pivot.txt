## I. The Strategic Pivot: Why Move?

The current setup with **Christian Community Credit Union (CCCU)** is functionally sound for basic operations but strategically limiting for a crypto-forward ministry.

*   **CCCU Limitations:**
    *   **Crypto-Averse:** No direct integration with exchanges; potential for "velocity flags" on frequent transfers to crypto platforms.
    *   **Legacy Infrastructure:** Slow ACH processing and lack of modern API integrations.
    *   **Hybrid Risk:** Mixing personal reimbursements with ministry funds creates a "piercing the veil" risk.

*   **The Target State:**
    *   **Crypto-Native:** A bank that understands and welcomes digital asset flows.
    *   **Unincorporated Friendly:** Recognizes the "unincorporated association" (EIN-only) structure without forcing corporate registration.
    *   **Sovereign Alignment:** A partner that respects privacy and resists "de-banking" based on political or religious views.

## Recurring Visual Elements

### Element 1: THE GREY (Alien Mascot)
- Classic grey alien silhouette — large black eyes, smooth grey skin, slender frame
- Wearing a simple golden sash or belt — the covenant marker
- Expression: calm, knowing, slightly amused
- Used in: Cold Open, Act One, Act Four, Close

### Element 2: THE COVENANT SCROLL
- Ancient parchment scroll, partially unrolled
- Hebrew/Ethiopic text visible on the surface
- Glowing golden light emanating from the text
- Morphs into / overlaid with smart contract code (green monospace text)
- Used in: Act One, Act Two

### Element 3: THE TEMPLE ARCHITECTURE
- Five stone columns rising from dark ground, lit from below with golden light
- Each column labeled (subtle, not garish): HEX / pTGC / pDAI / BEAR / pWBTC
- The innermost column (pWBTC) glows brightest — the holy of holies
- Used in: Act Five, Close

### Element 4: THE BLOCKCHAIN NODE NETWORK
- Dark background with glowing amber/gold nodes connected by thin light threads
- Nodes pulse and light up one by one as community grows
- Used in: Act Three

### Element 5: THE FIAT EMPIRE CONTRAST
- Cold grey/blue imagery — central bank building, dollar bill dissolving into ash
- Contrasted against warm gold covenant imagery
- Used in: Act Two (brief), Act Four
